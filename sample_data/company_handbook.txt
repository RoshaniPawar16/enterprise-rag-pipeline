CHAPTER 1: COMPANY OVERVIEW
==================================

ACME Corporation was founded in 2010 by Jane Smith and John Davis in London, UK.
The company specializes in enterprise software solutions for the healthcare and
financial services sectors.

Our mission is to simplify complex business processes through innovative technology.

Current employee count: 450 across 3 offices (London, Edinburgh, Manchester).
Annual revenue (2023): £45 million.

==================================
CHAPTER 2: EMPLOYMENT POLICIES
==================================

Working Hours
-------------
Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, Monday through Friday.
Core hours (when all employees must be available): 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
Flexible working arrangements are available upon manager approval.

Remote Work Policy
------------------
Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week after completing their
probation period (typically 3 months). Remote work requests must be submitted
through the HR portal at least 48 hours in advance.

Annual Leave
------------
- Standard entitlement: 25 days per year plus UK bank holidays
- After 3 years of service: 28 days per year
- After 5 years of service: 30 days per year

Unused leave can be carried over (maximum 5 days) to the following year.

Sick Leave
----------
Employees are entitled to full pay for up to 10 sick days per year.
A doctor's note is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

==================================
CHAPTER 3: COMPENSATION & BENEFITS
==================================

Salary Reviews
--------------
Salary reviews are conducted annually in April. Performance ratings directly
impact salary adjustments:
- Exceeds expectations: 5-8% increase
- Meets expectations: 2-4% increase
- Below expectations: No increase, performance improvement plan initiated

Pension Scheme
--------------
ACME offers a defined contribution pension scheme:
- Employee contribution: Minimum 5%
- Employer contribution: 8% (matching up to 10% if employee contributes 7%+)

Health Insurance
----------------
Comprehensive private health insurance is provided through Vitality Health.
Coverage includes:
- Medical treatment
- Dental care (up to £500/year)
- Optical care (up to £200/year)
- Mental health support (unlimited sessions)

==================================
CHAPTER 4: PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
==================================

Training Budget
---------------
Each employee has an annual training budget of £2,000 for:
- Conferences and workshops
- Online courses and certifications
- Books and learning materials

Unused budget does not carry over to the next year.

Mentorship Programme
--------------------
New employees are paired with a senior mentor for their first 6 months.
Mentors meet with mentees at least twice per month.

Career Progression
------------------
ACME uses a 6-level career framework:
- Level 1-2: Junior roles
- Level 3-4: Mid-level roles
- Level 5: Senior individual contributor or Team Lead
- Level 6: Director or Principal

Promotions are reviewed bi-annually (April and October).

==================================
CHAPTER 5: IT & SECURITY POLICIES
==================================

Password Requirements
---------------------
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Changed every 90 days
- No reuse of last 10 passwords

Data Classification
-------------------
All company data must be classified as:
- Public: Can be shared externally
- Internal: For ACME employees only
- Confidential: Restricted to specific teams
- Highly Confidential: Board and executive level only

GDPR Compliance
---------------
As a UK company, ACME fully complies with GDPR requirements.
Personal data training is mandatory for all employees annually.
Data breaches must be reported to IT Security within 24 hours.
